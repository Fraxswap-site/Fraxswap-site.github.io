Introduction to FraxSwap

FraxSwap is a decentralized, high-performance automated market maker (AMM) that enables permissionless, secure, and capital-efficient trading of digital assets across the Frax ecosystem and beyond. As a key innovation within the DeFi infrastructure, FraxSwap offers users advanced trading capabilities while maintaining low slippage and high composability.

What is FraxSwap?

FraxSwap is the first AMM with native Time-Weighted Average Market Maker (TWAMM) functionality. It allows traders to execute large orders over time, mitigating market impact while ensuring fully on-chain transparency. The platform is built with smart contract automation and optimal routing mechanisms.

  • On-chain TWAMM for large trade execution
  • Optimized routing and deep liquidity pools
  • Fully decentralized and composable with other protocols
  • Audited smart contracts and governance mechanisms

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – FraxSwap

Is FraxSwap Safe?

Yes. FraxSwap uses audited smart contracts and supports TWAMM to manage large trades securely and transparently.

What Assets Can I Swap?

You can trade FRAX, FXS, USDC, ETH, and other supported tokens on the platform.

How Long Do Swaps Take?

Instant execution for standard trades. TWAMM orders execute over time based on set intervals.

Are There Any Fees?

FraxSwap charges competitive swap fees that are displayed before execution.

Does It Support Hardware Wallets?

Yes, hardware wallets like Ledger and Trezor are fully supported.

Where Can I Monitor My Orders?

The FraxSwap dashboard provides real-time visibility into all executed and pending transactions.
